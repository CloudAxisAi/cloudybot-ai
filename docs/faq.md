# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

**What is CloudyBot?**
A hosted AI agent with a real cloud browser. Give it tasks in plain English via chat or WhatsApp — it browses, scrapes, fills forms, or answers questions from your documents.

**Do I need to install anything?**
No. Runs entirely in the cloud. Access via browser or WhatsApp.

**Who is CloudyBot for?**
Founders, sales teams, ops managers, and anyone who wants to automate repetitive web tasks without code.

---

## Features

**Can CloudyBot log into websites on my behalf?**
Yes. Credentials are stored encrypted and used only for your tasks.

**Does it work on JavaScript-heavy websites?**
Yes. Real Chromium browser — handles React, Vue, Angular the same way a human would.

**Can it handle multi-step tasks?**
Yes. "First find X, then for each result do Y, then compile into Z" — it maintains context throughout.

**Can I schedule recurring tasks?**
Yes. Daily, weekly, or custom schedule. CloudyBot runs automatically and notifies you.

**What file types does the knowledge base support?**
PDF, DOCX, TXT, and web URLs.

---

## Pricing

**How does hard-capped pricing work?**
You set a monthly cap. When you hit it, CloudyBot stops. No overages, no surprises.

**Is there a free tier?**
Yes. Limited tasks per month. No credit card required.

---

## Privacy & Security

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. Each session runs in an isolated container. Sessions destroyed after completion.

**Does CloudyBot store my browsing data?**
No. Browsing data not retained beyond task completion.

**Are my uploaded documents secure?**
Yes. Stored encrypted. Never shared or used to train models.

---

## Getting Started

**How do I start?**
Go to [cloudybot.ai](https://cloudybot.ai), sign up, type your first task.

**What's a good first task?**
> "Research the top 5 competitors of [your company] and summarise their pricing and key features."
