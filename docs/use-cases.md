# CloudyBot Use Cases

Real-world tasks that CloudyBot handles daily.

---

## Sales & Outreach

**LinkedIn Lead Research**
> "Find 50 CTOs at Series A fintech startups in London. Give me their names, company, LinkedIn URL, and company size."

**Personalised Outreach Drafting**
> "Go to each of these 20 LinkedIn profiles and draft a personalised connection request based on their recent posts."

**Cold Email Research**
> "Find the website, company description, and key product features for each company in this list."

---

## Market Research

**Competitor Analysis**
> "Research the top 7 competitors of [your product]. For each: pricing, key features, target audience, and positioning."

**Pricing Intelligence**
> "Monitor these 5 competitor pricing pages weekly and alert me when anything changes."

**Job Board Scraping**
> "Scrape all open engineering jobs at these 20 companies and give me a list with role, team, and requirements."

---

## Internal Knowledge Base

**Document Q&A**
Upload your company handbook, SOPs, contracts, or product docs. Then ask:
> "What is our refund policy?"
> "What does section 4.2 of the client contract say?"
> "Summarise the key points from last quarter's board deck."

**Onboarding Assistant**
Upload onboarding materials. New hires ask questions, CloudyBot answers from your actual docs.

---

## Content & SEO

**Topic Research**
> "Find the top 20 articles ranking for 'best AI tools for founders'. Summarise their key points and identify gaps."

**Backlink Prospecting**
> "Find blogs and newsletters that have linked to [competitor] in the last 6 months."

---

## Operations & Admin

**Form Automation**
> "Fill out this procurement form every month using this data."

**Web Monitoring**
> "Check this product page daily. Alert me when stock is available / price drops below £X."

**Data Entry**
> "Take each row in this spreadsheet, look up the company on Companies House, and fill in the registration number."

Go to [cloudybot.ai](https://cloudybot.ai) and paste any task above. No setup required.
