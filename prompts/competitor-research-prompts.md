# 20 Competitor Research Prompts (AI-Ready)

Copy-paste into your AI assistant. For anything that needs **live web pages** (pricing pages, review sites, news), use an agent with a **cloud browser** so you get current data, not training-cutoff guesses.

---

## Overview research

```
1. Give me a one-page summary of [competitor]: what they sell, who they target, pricing model (if public), and 3 recent moves from news or their blog in the last 90 days.

2. List [competitor]'s main products or modules. For each, one sentence on job-to-be-done and who it is for.

3. Who does [competitor] position against on their website? Pull exact phrases from their homepage and pricing page.

4. Summarize [competitor]'s funding, headcount signals, and hiring focus if visible on LinkedIn, Crunchbase, or their careers page (cite sources).
```

---

## Pricing analysis

```
5. Open [competitor]'s public pricing page. Extract every plan name, price, billing period, and stated limits (seats, API, storage, etc.) in a table.

6. Compare [competitor A] and [competitor B] pricing: cheapest paid tier, what it includes, and what triggers an upgrade.

7. Find any mention of enterprise, "contact sales," or custom pricing for [competitor]. Note what's hidden vs public.

8. Check [competitor]'s docs or changelog for pricing or limit changes in the last 12 months. List date and what changed.
```

---

## Content and SEO

```
9. From [competitor]'s site, list their top 10 blog or resource URLs by obvious prominence (nav, pillar pages, or sitemap if linked). For each, note the primary topic in one line.

10. Find [competitor]'s main SEO landing pages (e.g. /alternatives, /vs-*, /best-*). Summarize the angle of each.

11. Search for [competitor]'s guest posts or podcasts in the last 6 months. Return title, outlet, URL, and one-line takeaway.

12. What themes does [competitor] repeat across their homepage, blog, and social bios? List 5 recurring messages.
```

---

## Customer sentiment

```
13. Search G2, Capterra, or Trustpilot for [competitor]. Summarize the top 5 praise themes and top 5 complaint themes (with example quotes paraphrased, no PII).

14. Find Reddit or HN threads discussing [competitor] in the last year. What do users say they switched from/to, and why?

15. Look for public status incidents or outage history for [competitor]. Summarize frequency and how they communicate.

16. Find case studies or testimonials on [competitor]'s site. What industries and use cases do they emphasize?
```

---

## Market positioning

```
17. Compare [competitor A] and [competitor B] on: ideal customer, core differentiator, and pricing model — in a 3-column table.

18. If we sell [your product], draft a honest "when to choose us vs [competitor]" section for a sales deck (no trash talk, specific scenarios).

19. What integrations or ecosystem partners does [competitor] highlight? List categories (CRM, data, auth, etc.) and named tools if shown.

20. What regulatory, security, or compliance badges does [competitor] claim (SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)? List each and where it appears on their site.
```
