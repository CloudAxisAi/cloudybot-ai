# 20 Content Creation Prompts (AI-Ready)

Use with any capable LLM. When a prompt needs **fresh research, links, or on-site reading**, use an AI agent with **browser access** so outputs stay factual.

---

## Blog posts

```
1. Outline a 1,500-word blog post for [audience] on [topic]. Include: working title, 5 H2s, bullet points per section, and a suggested CTA.

2. Write 3 alternative opening paragraphs (hooks) for a post titled "[title]". Vary style: story, stat, contrarian take. Max 120 words each.

3. Turn these rough notes into a structured draft: [paste notes]. Target [tone]. Add H2s, short paragraphs, and one pull-quote.

4. Given keyword [primary] and secondary keywords [list], suggest meta title (≤60 chars), meta description (≤155 chars), and an H1 that reads naturally.

5. Write a conclusion for a blog about [topic] that summarizes 3 takeaways and offers one clear next step (no hard sell).
```

---

## Social content

```
6. Turn this blog summary into a LinkedIn post (max 220 words): [paste]. Lead with a hook, use short lines, end with one question.

7. Write a 5-tweet X/Twitter thread from this idea: [one sentence]. Number tweets, keep each under 260 characters, thread should stand alone without the blog.

8. Draft carousel slide copy (10 slides) for LinkedIn on [topic]: title slide, problem, 3 insights, example, CTA. One short line per slide.

9. Write 3 LinkedIn comments (thoughtful, non-spammy) to respond to a post that argues [summary of post]. Each under 400 characters.

10. Create 5 hook lines for short-form video or Reels about [topic] — first line only, pattern-interrupt style.
```

---

## Email newsletters

```
11. Subject line A/B test: write 6 subject lines for a newsletter about [theme]. Mix curiosity, direct benefit, and specificity.

12. Draft a weekly newsletter: intro (100 words), 3 curated links with one-line commentary each, and sign-off. Audience: [audience].

13. Write a re-engagement email for subscribers who have not opened in 60 days. Tone: friendly, one clear ask, easy unsubscribe respect.

14. Turn this announcement into a launch email: [paste facts]. Structure: hook, what's new, why it matters, single CTA button text + body CTA line.

15. Summarize [long article or URL description] into a "skim in 60 seconds" block for a newsletter with 3 bullets and one "read more" link placeholder.
```

---

## Repurposing

```
16. Convert this blog post into a LinkedIn post without sounding like a copy-paste: [paste or describe]. Max 200 words, new hook.

17. From this webinar transcript excerpt, extract: 7 tweet-sized insights + 1 thread opener: [paste excerpt].

18. Turn this case study into a 90-second spoken script for a founder update or podcast ad read: [paste key results].

19. Repackage this how-to article as a checklist (10–15 checkboxes) for a PDF lead magnet: [paste outline or article].

20. Given one long-form piece on [topic], propose a 4-week content calendar: blog, newsletter, 2 social posts, 1 repurposed thread — titles only.
```
