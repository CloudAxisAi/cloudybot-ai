# Example: Internal Knowledge Base with CloudyBot

Turn your company documents into an always-available Q&A assistant — no setup.

---

## The Problem

Your team has docs scattered everywhere. When someone has a question they interrupt a colleague or guess.

## The Solution

Upload documents to CloudyBot. Ask questions in plain English. Get precise answers with the source section cited.

---

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Upload**
Click Upload and add your files: PDF, DOCX, TXT, or paste a web URL.

**Step 2 — Ask**

```
What is our policy on remote work for employees based outside the UK?
```
```
What are the payment terms in the client services contract?
```
```
Summarise the key points from the Q3 board presentation.
```

**Step 3 — Share**
Anyone on your account queries the same knowledge base instantly.

---

## Real Example

**Uploaded:** 42-page HR policy document

**Question:** "Can employees take unpaid leave for personal reasons?"

**CloudyBot answer:**
> "Yes. Section 7.3 states that employees with more than 12 months tenure may request unpaid leave of up to 4 weeks per year. Requests must be submitted in writing to HR at least 4 weeks in advance and are subject to manager approval."

Exact answer. Section cited. No hallucination.
