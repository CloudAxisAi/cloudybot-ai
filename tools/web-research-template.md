# Web Research Brief Templates

Copy, fill in the brackets, paste into CloudyBot or any AI agent.

---

## Competitor Research

```
Research the top [NUMBER] competitors of [YOUR PRODUCT / CATEGORY].

For each return:
- Product name and URL
- Pricing (all tiers, or "contact for pricing" if not public)
- Top 5 features
- Target audience
- One-sentence positioning statement
- Average rating on G2 or Capterra
- Most common complaint from reviews

Format as a comparison table.
Add a "Gaps & Opportunities" section at the end.
```

---

## Industry News

```
Find the top [NUMBER] news stories about [TOPIC / INDUSTRY] published in the last [TIMEFRAME].

For each return:
- Headline
- Publication name and date
- URL
- 2-sentence summary
- Why it matters for [YOUR COMPANY / AUDIENCE]

Sort by relevance, most important first.
```

---

## Lead List Research

```
Find [NUMBER] [JOB TITLE]s at [INDUSTRY] companies in [LOCATION].

For each return:
- Full name, job title, company name, company size
- LinkedIn URL
- One sentence about what their company does

Only include people who appear to be active on LinkedIn.
Format as a table.
```

---

## Tool Evaluation

```
I'm evaluating tools for [USE CASE]. Research the best options available in 2026.

For each tool return:
- Tool name and URL
- Pricing
- Key features relevant to [USE CASE]
- Pros (top 3) and Cons (top 3)
- Best for (type of user)

At the end, give a recommendation based on: [YOUR PRIORITIES]
```

---

## Funding / Market Research

```
Find all [FUNDING STAGE] rounds in [INDUSTRY] in [LOCATION] in the last [TIMEFRAME].

For each return:
- Company name, amount raised, investors
- Company description (1 sentence)
- Source URL

Sort by amount raised, largest first.
```

---

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Specify count** — "find 20" beats "find some"
2. **Name the output format** — "as a table" or "as a bullet list"
3. **Add a constraint** — "only companies with 10–200 employees"
4. **Ask for sources** — "include the URL for each item"
5. **Chain tasks** — "now take the top 3 and research them in more detail"
